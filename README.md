## 🧠 About Me


Hey, I'm **Lakshit** — a Data Science undergrad from Bhilwara, Rajasthan 🇮🇳, who got hooked on AI the moment a model I trained actually predicted something right.

I spend my days (and honestly, too many nights) building systems that learn — from classical ML pipelines to full-stack LLM applications with RAG. I'm not just interested in *using* AI tools; I want to understand what's under the hood, break things, fix them, and ship something real.

My sweet spot is the intersection of **machine learning**, **data engineering**, and **LLM systems** — where messy raw data transforms into something that genuinely solves a problem.

When I'm not deep in a Jupyter notebook or debugging a vector database query, I'm writing about what I learn, exploring open-source projects, or convincing myself that *one more paper* before bed is a great idea. 📄
